Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose a mentor?

Use the filters on Find Mentors, read profiles, and book a low-cost intro session.

Do you offer financial aid?

Yes—partial and full session support. See Student Hub to apply.

Where do sessions happen?

Online via your favorite tool or in-person at agreed study spaces.

Are mentors verified?

We check identity, references, sample lessons, and communication style.

Can I bring a friend?

Yes—book a small-group slot to share the cost.

Do you support schools?

We set up cohorts for classes and clubs. Contact us for details.
